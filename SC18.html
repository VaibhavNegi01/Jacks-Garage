Brief overview about this car

SC18 The SC18 Alston is the first one-off in the history of the Sant’Agata Bolognese motorsport division. This unique specimen was designed in conjunction with the customer and the Lamborghini Centro Stile. It is road approved but designed primarily for use on the track. The car features extreme aerodynamics that were developed specifically for the model and based on Squadra Corse’s competitive experience. The racing features start with the front hood, which boasts air intakes in the style of the Huracán GT3 EVO. They continue with the sides and rear, where there are fenders, fins and air scoops inspired by the Huracán Super Trofeo EVO. Rounding off the aerodynamic system is a large carbon fibre wing with three mechanical settings that can generate optimum aerodynamic load on any circuit. In addition, borrowing a tried and tested solution from endurance races, twelve air intakes have been formed on the rear hood to increase heat exchange and showcase the V12 Lamborghini engine.
